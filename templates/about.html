Our Story

Born from a Passion for Education

EduCraft Solutions was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to make quality education accessible to every ambitious student, regardless of where they come from.

Operating under the Apex Abroad Overseas brand for international services and P Shiksha 4.0 for domestic academic excellence, we have become a trusted name for research support, overseas admissions, and student success.

With our head office in New Delhi and a network of counselors nationwide, we have guided over 5,000 students to universities in 20+ countries.
